Frequently Asked Questions

Manage FAQ articles for customer self-service.

How do I reset my password?

To reset your password, click on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page. Enter your email address, and we'll send you a password reset link. Follow the instructions in the email to create a new password.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, and bank transfers. For recurring subscriptions, we also support automatic monthly billing.

How can I track my order?

Once your order ships, you'll receive a tracking number via email. You can also log into your account and view your order history to see the latest tracking information.

Do you offer refunds?

Yes, we offer a 30-day money-back guarantee on all products. If you're not satisfied, contact our support team to initiate a refund.

How do I contact customer support?

You can contact us via email at support@connectly.com, through our live chat, or by submitting a ticket in the support center. We aim to respond within 24 hours.
